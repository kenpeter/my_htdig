Elsewhere: The Road Movie
Related EntriesAudio Visual Gallery
Gallery Installation and Temporary Installation
Location: Queensland University of Technology & Institute of Modern Art, Queensland, Australia
By Nola Farman
Details
An armchair is clad with shards of broken crockery. The chair is at once attractive and repulsive. This work was made while Artist in Residence at the Queensland University of Technology.
The chair sits in front of a TV from which an excess of sound pours out. The red light makes the room seem hot. The floor and other pieces of furniture are also covered with crockery.
