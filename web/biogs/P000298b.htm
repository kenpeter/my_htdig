Jercher, Herbert
Related Entries
Artist, Improviser, Instrument Maker, Sound Scupltor and Composer
Herbert is a Sound Artist who designs and constructs acoustic play instruments. His sound sculptures encourage interaction by people of all ages and abilities. His work is featured by prominent landscape architects in public and private spaces thoughout Australia. Herb is a multi-discilpined artist working as a Guitarist, Composer, Performance Artist, Sound Sculptor and Educator.
