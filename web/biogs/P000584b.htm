Miranda, Maria
Related Entries
Visual Artist and Multimedia Artist
URL: The home page for this entity is located at http://www.out-of-sync.com/
Maria Miranda (aka Max) is a visual/new media artist. She has a BA in Linguistics from the Australian National University, Canberra. (1987) and studied graphic design at Randwick College of TAFE in Sydney. Recently (2003) she completed her MVA at Sydney College of the Arts focusing on the fictive in new media. She has worked as a graphic designer and drawn comix. In 1989 she co-edited Drawing Away: an Australian women's comic book.

For the last ten years most of my work has been made in collaboration with Norie Neumark. We have called this collaboration Out-of-Sync, which is a good description of how we work.
