Subterranean Listening Device
Related EntriesAudio Visual Gallery
Outdoor Installation
Location: Mundaring Sculpture Park, Mundaring, Western Australia, Australia
By Nola Farman
Details
A seismometer, which is buried in the ground, sends signals of earth tremors to a sound system. This in turn sends the sound of the vibrations to a number of listening stations at the surface. The work is solar powered.
