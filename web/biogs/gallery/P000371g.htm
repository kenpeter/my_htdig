Carpark Plan
Title:Carpark Plan
Type:image
Description:

Map shows the second level of a three level Car Park servicing Federation Square. Transient Frequencies is located on this second level as shown by the six speaker units, which also contain infrared detectors. Movement is registered at these six points in the Car Park drop-off area with this information being sent to the Matrix processor situated in the Boiler Room. The Matrix system then generates a random sonic sequence that is disseminated across the six speaker unit positions.
