Natural Rhythm For Underwater Sounds & Electronics - Audio Excerpt 1
Title:Natural Rhythm For Underwater Sounds & Electronics - Audio Excerpt 1
Type:MP3 File
Description:

Binaural recording - best heard on headphones: Sounds include: 1) Clicking of shrimp 2) Electronic pitches in reply to shrimp 3) Sizzzz of motorboats underwater sound 4) Waves 5) Water gong 6) Wiggle of oscillators tracing amplitude of hydrophone output 7) Debussy's "La Mer" played under water and processed by waves 8) General public sounds 9) Warren Burt talking to the public 10) seagulls
