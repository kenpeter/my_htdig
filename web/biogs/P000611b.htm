SPINE 1.2 (2006)
Related Entries
Location: Union Lane, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
Installation by Gina Czarnecki
Details
In a narrow crevice between monolithic stores in the heart of Melbourne’s shopping precinct, thousands of bodies move with smoke-like fluidity up and down a 6-metrehigh video screen. The mesmeric vertical grace of the work sits in stark contrast to the horizontal walkways conveying traffic between temples of retail activity - human traffic of a different kind.

The specially commissioned sound is an integral component of how the work unfolds, the images merge and mutate into a form resembling - from a distance - a human vertebrae.

28 Jun - 23 Aug 06 (dusk to midnight)

Gina Czarnecki, Spine
Produced by forma, www.forma.org.uk
Commissioned by AV festival 06
