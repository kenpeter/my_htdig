Audio Only Sound Gallery
Related EntriesAudio Visual Gallery
AO: Audio Only sound galley was a 2 hour exhibition curated by Andrew Kettle for MAAP 01 including artists from Taiwan, Malaysia, China, New Zealand and Australia. Philip Samartzis, Fujui Wang, Yin Pin, Alan Nguyen, Jennifer Sochackyj, Andrew Kettle, Wangwei, Alex Monteith, Bruce Russell & Chin-Chin Chen. Ao: Audio only utilises 'visual deprivation'. In the darkened, surround-sound theatre the audience where encouraged to place blindfolds over their eyes to enhance the dependancy and hense the appreciation of the audio program. Seating was arranged in to concentric rings surrounded in four corners with speakers. Brisbane Powerhouse, Graffiti Room. 12-14 October, 2001. The Sound Gallery also included the exhibitions 'Sound Spaces' (Totally Huge New Music Festival, Perth), 'Sound2' (Beijing), 'Transmit' (Melbourne) & D>Art01 (Dlux, Sydney).
